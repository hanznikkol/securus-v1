About the website

Understanding something about the human brain can be complicated to some people sometimes. They need to be guided and educated to be more aware of their surroundings and their way of interacting with others because they may trigger something in them that can lead to a panic or anxiety attack. We should make sure to make people feel safe around us.


The developers made the website with the purpose of helping people cope with their attacks and spread information about panic attack and anxiety attack. They included basic information about the two topics for further understanding. They also made sure to insert breathing exercises that might help those who are currently experiencing an attack. And lastly, the website also contains a contact info wherein you can know how, where, and who to ask for help, in case you’re finding for someone to listen to your worries.


The developers are going to make sure that you will be heard. You’re not alone! There will always be someone who’s willing to help you. You matter.
