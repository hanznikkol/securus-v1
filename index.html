Know the differences between Panic and Anxiety Attack

Panic attack and anxiety attack have their differences but people often mistake them as the same thing. Learn about their differences, symptoms, causes, and ways on how it can be stopped.

We provide breathing exercises that can help you to calm down

Attacks are hard to stop especially when you’re on your own. It would be hard to calm down if you do not know what you should do. You need breathing exercises! We provide GIFs and animations that can somehow help you calm down. We also included de-stressing techniques, encouraging quotes, and motivational videos.
